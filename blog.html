Psychology Library

Comprehensive guides on the 10 personality factors measured in our assessment. Written for educational purposes.

Narcissism

Grandiosity, need for admiration, and lack of empathy.

Borderline

Emotional instability, fear of abandonment, and intense relationships.

Depressive

Persistently low mood, energy loss, and feelings of inadequacy.

Anxiety

Excessive worry, tension, and anticipation of future threats.

Compulsive

Preoccupation with orderliness, perfectionism, and control.

Avoidant

Social inhibition, feelings of inadequacy, and hypersensitivity.

Paranoid

Distrust and suspicion of others' motives without sufficient basis.

Histrionic

Excessive emotionality and attention-seeking behavior.

Schizoid

Detachment from social relationships and limited emotional expression.

Aggressive

Hostility, irritability, and tendency towards conflict or domination.
